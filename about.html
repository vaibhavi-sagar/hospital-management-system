About FoodShare

FoodShare is a community-driven food donation platform that helps connect food donors with NGOs, orphanages, shelters, and people in need. Every day, large amounts of perfectly edible food are wasted while many people go hungry. Our mission is to bridge this gap using technology.

Our Mission

To reduce food waste and ensure surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Our Vision

To build a hunger-free society where food is shared instead of wasted.

Community

Connecting restaurants, individuals, NGOs, and volunteers through one platform.
